## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Vietnamese Content
- Professional but approachable
- Use formal Vietnamese legal terminology
- Emphasize trust and expertise
- Target: Vietnamese citizens seeking legal guidance

### English Content
- Professional and clear
- Avoid overly complex legal jargon
- Target: Foreigners living/working in Ho Chi Minh City
- Focus on practical, actionable advice
