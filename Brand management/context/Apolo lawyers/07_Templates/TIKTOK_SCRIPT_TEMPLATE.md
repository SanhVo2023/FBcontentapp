## TIPS FOR TIKTOK SUCCESS

1. **First 1-3 seconds** = most important (hook must be strong)
2. **Face on camera** = higher engagement
3. **Captions always** = most watch without sound
4. **Trending sounds** = boost visibility
5. **Post consistently** = algorithm rewards regular posting
6. **Reply to comments with videos** = extra content + engagement
